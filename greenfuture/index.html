About Us

Green Cape Town is a student-led initiative working with local communities to plant trees, reduce waste, and raise awareness about sustainable living. Founded in 2025, we are committed to measurable impact and grassroots involvement.

Our Campaigns

Urban Tree Drive

Monthly tree-planting events across local schools and parks.

Zero-Waste Challenge

Workshops and campaigns to reduce single-use plastics and promote recycling.

Green Schools

Partnering with schools to teach students about sustainability and eco-friendly practices.
